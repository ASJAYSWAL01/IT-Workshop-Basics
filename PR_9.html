LDRP Institute of Technology and Research


LDRP Institute of Technology and Research, Gandhinagar was established in 2005-2006. The college has made steady progress during the past years of its existence.

It has played a vital role among engineering colleges of Gujarat. Today it offers several B.E., M.B.A., and M.C.A. programs.

LDRP-ITR, Gandhinagar is a constituent Institute of Kadi Sarva Vishwavidyalaya.

Courses Offered

  • Computer Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Automobile Engineering
  • Information Technology Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
